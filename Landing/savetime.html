Save Time with RideShare KPU

Managing your time is crucial as a busy student. RideShare KPU helps you cut down your commute time by sharing rides with fellow students.

How Ride Sharing Saves You Time

  • Carpool Lanes Access: Use high-occupancy vehicle lanes to avoid traffic congestion.
  • Priority Parking: Get access to designated carpool parking spots close to campus entrances.
  • Coordinated Schedules: Share rides with students who have similar class times to minimize wait times.
  • Stress-Free Commutes: Relax and socialize during your ride instead of driving alone.

Typical Time Savings

Students using RideShare KPU report saving 10 to 20 minutes per trip on average, adding valuable time back to their day for study, work, or rest.

Getting Started is Easy

  1. Create a RideShare KPU account with your student email.
  2. Search or post your preferred routes and schedules.
  3. Connect with students who match your timing.
  4. Coordinate rides seamlessly with in-app messaging.
  5. Enjoy your time-saving commute!
