Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Here’s everything you need to know about RideShare KPU.

❓ What is RideShare KPU?

✅ RideShare KPU is a carpooling platform exclusively for KPU students to connect and share rides between campuses and nearby areas, saving money and reducing environmental impact.

❓ Is RideShare KPU free to use?

✅ Yes! Signing up and using the platform is completely free. You only split commuting costs like fuel or tolls with your ride partners.

❓ How do I sign up?

✅ Simply click on Sign Up and register with your verified KPU student email. Complete your profile to start finding or offering rides.

❓ Is RideShare KPU safe?

✅ Absolutely. Only verified KPU students can register, and our system includes reviews and ratings to help you choose reliable ride partners.

❓ Can I be both a driver and a passenger?

✅ Yes! You can list your own ride as a driver or search for available rides as a passenger anytime.

❓ How do I find rides?

✅ Once logged in, browse the available rides by campus, date, or route. You can also post your own ride request if you don’t see one that fits.

❓ How do I offer rides?

✅ Go to the “Offer a Ride” section in your dashboard, enter your route, available seats, and schedule, and publish it for others to join.

❓ How are costs shared?

✅ Costs are agreed upon between the driver and passengers. Most students simply split gas or parking costs equally.

❓ Can I schedule rides in advance?

✅ Yes! Post or search for rides days or weeks ahead. Early planning helps you secure your preferred route and companions.

❓ What if I need to cancel a ride?

✅ We understand plans change. Just notify your driver or passengers as soon as possible through our in-app messaging system.

❓ Can I ride between different campuses?

✅ Yes! Popular routes include Surrey ↔ Richmond, Langley ↔ Surrey, and many others. You can post or join rides on any campus route.

❓ Who can I contact for help?

✅ Our support team is here to help! Email us at rideshare@kpu.ca or visit our Contact page for assistance.

Still Have Questions?

We’re here for you! Reach out to our support team or explore more on our platform.
