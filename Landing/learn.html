Learn More About RideShare KPU

Your trusted platform for convenient, eco-friendly rides between KPU campuses.

What is RideShare KPU?

RideShare KPU is a carpooling platform dedicated to Kwantlen Polytechnic University students and staff. It helps you find rides, offer your own, and connect with fellow campus travelers easily and securely.

Benefits of Using RideShare KPU

  • Save money: Share fuel costs and reduce expenses.
  • Reduce carbon footprint: Contribute to sustainability by carpooling.
  • Meet new people: Connect with fellow KPU students and staff.
  • Flexible rides: Find rides that fit your schedule or offer rides on your terms.
  • Safety first: Built-in messaging and verified user profiles to ensure trust.

How to Get Started

  1. Create your profile and verify your KPU affiliation.
  2. Browse upcoming rides and request to join one.
  3. Offer your own ride and share your trip details.
  4. Communicate securely through the platform messaging system.
  5. Confirm rides and enjoy hassle-free transportation.

Ride Safety Tips and Resources

  • Verify profiles: Always check user verification and reviews before accepting or offering rides.
  • Communicate clearly: Use the platform’s messaging system to discuss meeting points and trip details.
  • Share your ride plan: Inform a trusted friend or family member about your ride details and schedule.
  • Meet in public places: Arrange pick-up and drop-off points in safe, well-lit, and public locations.
  • Follow traffic laws: Always obey road rules and drive responsibly to ensure safety for everyone.
  • Report concerns: If you experience any issues or suspicious behavior, report immediately to RideShare KPU support.

For more detailed safety guidelines, visit the KPU Safety Resources page.
