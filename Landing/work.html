How RideShare KPU Works

Getting started with RideShare KPU is simple. Follow these steps to find or offer rides, connect with verified students, and enjoy a safer, greener commute.

1️⃣

Create Your Account

Sign up using your KPU student email to ensure that only verified students join our platform. Your account will give you access to search and post rides across all campuses.

2️⃣

Search or Offer a Ride

Use the search feature to look for available rides heading to your campus. Alternatively, post your own ride to share with others. You can filter by campus, time, and preferred routes.

3️⃣

Connect with Students

Once you’ve found a match, chat directly with the student to confirm details. All profiles include KPU verification and reviews from past rides for added peace of mind.

4️⃣

Share Your Ride

Meet up at your agreed location, enjoy the ride, and split the cost. It’s that simple! You can also rate your ride after each trip to help maintain a trusted community.

Extra Features You’ll Love

  • ✅ Real-time ride updates and notifications
  • ✅ Filter rides by time, campus, or preferences
  • ✅ Eco-friendly badges for frequent sharers
  • ✅ Built-in review system to keep rides safe and trusted
