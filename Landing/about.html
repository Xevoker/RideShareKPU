About RideShare KPU

RideShare KPU is a dedicated carpooling platform created exclusively for students of Kwantlen Polytechnic University. Our mission is to provide a safe, convenient, and eco-friendly way for students across all campuses to connect and share rides.

Our Mission

We strive to reduce the environmental impact of commuting by encouraging shared transportation, cutting down on traffic congestion, and promoting a culture of sustainability on campus. We aim to empower KPU students to save money, reduce carbon footprints, and build a stronger campus community through convenient ride-sharing.

Why Choose RideShare KPU?

  • Exclusive for KPU Students: Only verified KPU students can join the platform, ensuring a trusted and secure community.
  • Cost-Effective: Save money on gas, parking fees, and transit costs by sharing rides with fellow students.
  • Environmental Impact: Fewer cars on the road means less pollution and a healthier campus environment.
  • Campus Connectivity: Easily find rides between Surrey, Richmond, Langley, Civic Plaza, and Tech campuses.
  • User-Friendly Platform: A simple interface designed with students in mind, making ride matching quick and easy.

How We Ensure Safety

Safety is our top priority. All members must verify their KPU student status before joining the platform. Additionally, users can review and rate their ride experiences, helping maintain a trusted and reliable community.

Community Impact

Since launching, RideShare KPU has connected thousands of students, facilitating over 3,500 shared rides and saving the community significant travel costs and emissions. We continuously work to improve our services based on student feedback to enhance the ride-sharing experience for all users.
