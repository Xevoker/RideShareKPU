What is RideShare KPU?

RideShare KPU is a smart, sustainable ride-sharing platform for KPU students. We connect verified students to share rides safely, split commuting costs, and reduce the environmental impact of daily travel. Whether you're commuting to Surrey, Richmond, Langley, or Civic Plaza campuses — we've got you covered.

Why RideShare KPU?

💰 Save Money

Cut down on gas, parking, and transit expenses by splitting the cost with fellow students.

🚗 Ease Parking

Fewer cars on campus means less parking stress and lower parking fees.

🔒 Safer Rides

Ride only with verified KPU students in a trusted, secure platform.

🌱 Go Green

Reduce emissions and contribute to a cleaner, greener campus.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your free account using your KPU student email.

2. Connect

Search for rides or list your own. Connect with verified students heading your way.

3. Share

Coordinate rides, share costs, and enjoy a safer, greener commute.

Frequently Asked Questions

🔐 Is RideShare KPU safe?

Yes! Only verified KPU students can join, ensuring trusted connections.

💲 Do I have to pay to join?

No, RideShare KPU is free for all students. You only split commuting costs with your ride partners.

📅 Can I schedule rides in advance?

Absolutely! Post your ride ahead of time and coordinate with interested students.
