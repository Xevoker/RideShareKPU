How RideShare Keeps You Safe

Your safety is our top priority. We designed RideShare KPU with features that ensure you always travel with trusted peers and stay in control of your commute.

Every member signs up using a KPU email, ensuring rides are shared only within our student community.

📱 Secure Messaging

Communicate safely within the platform without sharing personal contact details until you’re ready.

Leave feedback on drivers and passengers to keep our community accountable and trustworthy.
