Build Connections Through Ride Sharing

RideShare KPU isn't just about getting from point A to B. It's about building a community of students who support each other, save money, and reduce environmental impact together.

Why Building Connections Matters

  • Friendship & Networking: Meet fellow students with shared schedules and interests.
  • Safety & Trust: Ride with verified KPU students you can rely on.
  • Support System: Share tips, study groups, and campus insights during your commute.
  • Community Impact: Promote sustainability and reduce traffic congestion on campus.

How RideShare KPU Builds Connections

  1. Create and customize your profile so others know who you are.
  2. Find rides or offer your own based on your schedule.
  3. Message and coordinate securely within the platform.
  4. Rate and review your ride experiences to help others choose trusted partners.
  5. Join community events and discussion forums hosted by RideShare KPU.
