How RideShare KPU Works

Discover how we help KPU students connect, share rides, and build a sustainable community.

Step-by-Step Guide

1️⃣ Create an Account

Sign up with your verified KPU email and set up your student profile.

2️⃣ Find or Offer Rides

Search for rides posted by fellow students or offer your own ride to share.

3️⃣ Connect & Coordinate

Use our secure chat system to plan details, pickup points, and schedules.

4️⃣ Enjoy the Ride

Save on costs, reduce carbon emissions, and make new friends along the way!

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is RideShare KPU free to use?

✅ Yes! Our platform is free for all verified KPU students. You only share costs with your ride partners.

❓ Is it safe?

✅ Absolutely. Every user must sign up with a valid KPU email, and our team monitors activity regularly.

❓ Can I be both a driver and a passenger?

✅ Yes! You can switch roles anytime based on your schedule and needs.
