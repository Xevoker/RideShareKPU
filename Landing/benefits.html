Why RideShare KPU?

Joining RideShare KPU gives you multiple advantages — both for your wallet and the environment. Check out the key benefits below:

💰 Save Money

Cut down on gas, parking, and transit expenses by splitting the cost with fellow KPU students.

🚗 Ease Parking

Fewer cars on campus means less parking stress and lower parking fees.

🔒 Safer Rides

Ride only with verified KPU students in a trusted, secure platform.

🌱 Go Green

Reduce emissions and contribute to a cleaner, greener campus and planet.

👥 Build Connections

Meet new classmates and build community while commuting together.

⏰ Save Time

Enjoy carpool lanes and reduce the stress of driving solo during rush hours.
