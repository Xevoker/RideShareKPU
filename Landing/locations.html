Popular Campus Routes

RideShare KPU connects students across all campuses. Below are some of the most popular routes students share rides on. Whether you’re heading to class, a lab, or an event — we’ve got you covered.

🏫 Surrey Main Campus ↔ Richmond Campus

One of the busiest routes, perfect for students attending classes in both Surrey and Richmond.

🏫 Surrey Main Campus ↔ Langley Campus

A popular route for students who live in Surrey but attend programs at Langley.

🏫 Langley Campus ↔ Richmond Campus

Save time and parking fees by sharing rides between Langley and Richmond.

🏫 Langley Campus ↔ Civic Plaza Campus

A convenient route for students commuting between Langley and Civic Plaza.

🏫 Civic Plaza Campus ↔ Richmond Campus

Connect with students traveling between Civic Plaza and Richmond with ease.

🏫 Tech Campus ↔ Surrey Main Campus

A frequent route for trades and tech students — ride together and reduce emissions.
