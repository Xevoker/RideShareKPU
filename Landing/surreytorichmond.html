Richmond Campus

This is one of the most popular routes among KPU students, connecting two major campuses: Surrey Main and Richmond. Whether you’re attending classes, labs, or campus events, RideShare KPU makes commuting between these locations easy, affordable, and eco-friendly.

Why Choose This Route?

  • Cost Savings: Share gas and parking expenses with fellow students.
  • Time Efficiency: Reduce your commute time by carpooling and accessing priority parking.
  • Community: Meet and connect with other students who share your schedule.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Lower your carbon footprint by reducing the number of cars on the road.

Typical Commute Info

The drive between Surrey Main Campus and Richmond Campus typically takes around 25-35 minutes, depending on traffic. Carpooling helps you avoid parking hassles and reduces wear on your vehicle.

How to Get Started

  1. Sign up for a free RideShare KPU account using your student email.
  2. Search for available rides or post your own commute for Surrey ↔ Richmond.
  3. Connect securely with verified KPU students heading your way.
  4. Coordinate ride times and locations easily through the platform.
  5. Enjoy your shared ride and save money!
